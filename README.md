## nibble — turn your reading backlog into a daily habit

Nibble is a Chrome extension for the chronically over-subscribed. Star newsletters, articles, and links as they arrive in your email, and Nibble surfaces one read each day so your backlog never feels overwhelming. Every article you finish helps nourish and grow your garden, turning consistent reading into a cute lil' reward. :)
